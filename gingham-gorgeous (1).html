The Profile

What does it smell like?

Scent Family Floral Fruity
Best Season Spring & Summer ☀️
Occasion Daytime · Brunch · Class
Vibe Feminine · Bright · Playful

Opens sweet and fruity with a burst of strawberry and peach, then softens into a floral heart of peonies — like a bouquet of flowers in every room of your home. Think: picnic in a flower garden, light and airy, very "pretty girl spring."

Layer It

Best lotion pairings

✨

Gingham Gorgeous Body Lotion

Perfect match — same line, same notes, maximum longevity. This is the go-to.

🌸

Japanese Cherry Blossom

Adds soft floral depth without overpowering the peach and strawberry top notes.

🌙

Into the Night

Creates sultry contrast — floral by day, mysterious base underneath. Great for evenings out.

🤍

Moonlight Path

Soft, clean musk grounds the fruity top notes beautifully. Elegant and effortless.

🍦

Warm Vanilla Sugar

Warms everything up and makes it cozier — perfect for a fall transition.

Pro tip: Apply lotion first, let it absorb, then spray — your scent will last 2–3× longer.

Mix & Match

Layering combos

01
Soft Girl Summer 🍑
Gingham Gorgeous + Pink Obsession

Strawberry + peach + pink notes = ultra feminine, bright, and sweet. Very "clean girl" energy. Perfect for a hot day out.

02
Champagne Picnic 🥂
Gingham Gorgeous + Champagne Toast

The bubbly, citrusy champagne lifts the peony and peach into something effervescent and festive. Great for going out.

03
A Thousand Wishes Glow-Up ✨
Gingham Gorgeous + A Thousand Wishes

ATW adds sparkling champagne, almond, and peach. Combined with Gorgeous, you get a dreamy, almost luxury fruity floral. The most elevated combo.

04
Floral Fantasy 🌺
Gingham Gorgeous + Japanese Cherry Blossom

Doubles down on the floral while adding a powdery softness. Very romantic and delicate — a bouquet in a bottle.
